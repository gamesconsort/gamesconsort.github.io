Modules

The CGI training will be delivered via live instructor-led sessions, pre-recorded lectures, hands-on labs, and a variety of assessments. The curriculum for this track is subdivided into 4 parts and you will take all the modules in them.

Part I: BA Foundations

  • 1. Introduction to Data and Analytics
  • 2. The Data Value Chain: Decisions, Operations, Monetisation
  • 3. Data Strategy Design and Execution
  • 4. Business Thinking for Analysts
  • 5. Systems Thinking for Analysts
  • 6. Success Factors for Business Analytics
  • 7. BA at the Strategic and Functional Levels
  • 8. Paradigms in Business Analytics
  • 9. Business Analytics Project Lifecycle

II: Working with Data

  • 1. Python Programming Basics
  • 2. Python Stack for Data Science
  • 3. Introduction to Stats and Probability
  • 4. Data Structures and Algorithms
  • 5. Cloud Data Architectures
  • 6. Databases and SQL Fundamentals
  • 7. Descriptive Analytics: Introduction
  • 8. Data Acquisition and Web Scraping
  • 9. Data Cleaning and Manipulation
  • 10. Data Prep with Paxata on DataRobot

III: Uncovering Business Insights

  • 1. Statistics and Exploratory Data Analysis
  • 2. Probability Distributions
  • 3. Statistical Inference: Parameter Estimation
  • 4. Statistical Inference: Hypothesis Testing
  • 5. Statistical Inference: Regression Analysis
  • 6. Decision Making Under Uncertainty
  • 7. EDA and Business Data Analysis in SQL
  • 8. Machine Learning and Pattern Discovery

IV: Communicating Business Insights

  • 1. Storytelling and Communication with Data
  • 2. Business Reporting with SQL Dashboards
  • 3. Data Visualisation with Power BI
  • 4. Interactive Visualisation and Power BI Best Practices
  • 5. Communicating Decision Insights
  • 6. Pitching to Managers and Decision Makers
  • 7. Understanding Stakeholder Management
