Career Growth Incubator – CGI


The CGI programme is a technology incubator learning experience designed to lower the barrier to entry into amazing tech careers. At extremely subsidised costs, the CGI pathways furnish you with the comprehensive blend of skills you need to have a powerful headstart in the tech space. After successful training you will have the option to progress into the Career Growth Accelerator programme to strengthen your skillset and become a competitive talent.

The CGI training runs for 3 months after which you will have the opportunity to join our Career Growth Accelerator (CGA) for another 3 months reinforcing your skills and building workplace readiness.

Progress from CGI 
to CGA


Our Career Growth Accelerator (CGA) internship consists of further training activities and a remote work component that will help you further your exerience navigating complex industry problems, building interesting projects, and strengthening communication and collaboration skills.
