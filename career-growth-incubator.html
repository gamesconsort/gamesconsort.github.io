Career Growth Incubator


The CGI programme is a tech incubator learning experience designed to lower the barrier to entry into amazing tech careers. At extremely subsidised costs, the CGI pathways furnish you with the comprehensive blend of skills you need to have a powerful headstart in the tech space. After successful training you will have the option to progress into the Career Growth Accelerator programme to strengthen your skillset and become a competitive talent.
