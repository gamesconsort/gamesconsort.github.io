CGA Internships


The Career Growth Accelerator (CGA) programme is an experiential learning opportunity designed to help more and more people break into the tech space. The internship component is a blend of comprehensive online training and minimal remote work running for 6 months. This rigorous and demanding programme will furnish you with the full range of skills you need to become a competitive talent in both the tech and business world.
