CGA INTERNSHIP

Data Science


Get the intensive training and practical experience you need for a successful career in data science.

Applications are now closed

At a glance


The CGA Internship programme in data science is an immersive 6-month experiential learning opportunity that equips you with a blend of sophisticated data skills, cross-industry domain understanding, and workplace readiness.

As a CGA Data Intern, you will receive comprehensive training in data science and reinforce your skills through continuous practice as you work on several projects. We will take you on an aggressive journey of growth and mastery, furnishing you with the practical skillset required to become a competitive talent in the tech space.

Training Outline

The CGA internship curriculum has been carefully designed to help you significantly grow your knowledge base in data science over 6 months. The training will be delivered via instructor-led live sessions, pre-recorded lectures, hands-on labs, and a variety of assessments.

Eligibility Requirements


1. All interested candidates must

Possess good communication ability, and basic knowledge of how to use a computer and the internet.

Possess a PC (with functional mic and webcam) with which they can participate in online sessions and activities.
Be able to demonstrate strong motivation for data science, by attending and passing a short online screening course in data fundamentals.


2. All selected CGA interns must

Be able to devote the next 6 months (starting August) to the internship which includes comprehensive online training and remote work activities.

Be able to commit at least 20 hours every week to all activities and programmes in the internship.
Have access to steady internet (at least 20GB per month).
