CGA INTERNSHIP

Data Science


Get the intensive training and practical experience you need for a successful career in data science.

At a glance


The CGA Internship programme in data science is an immersive 6-month experiential learning opportunity that equips you with a blend of sophisticated data skills, cross-industry domain understanding, and workplace readiness.

As a CGA Data Intern, you will receive comprehensive training in data science and reinforce your skills through continuous practice as you work on several projects. We will take you on an aggressive journey of growth and mastery, furnishing you with the practical skillset required to become a competitive talent in the tech space.

TOOLS YOU WILL LEARN


Training Outline

The CGA internship curriculum has been carefully designed to help you significantly grow your knowledge base in data science over 6 months. The training will be delivered via instructor-led live sessions, pre-recorded lectures, hands-on labs, and a variety of assessments.

I: Breaking into Data Science

  • 1. Introduction to Data and Analytics
  • 2. The Data Value Chain: Decisions, Operations, Monetisation
  • 3. Business Thinking for Data Scientists
  • 4. Data Strategy Design and Execution
  • 5. Data Science Project Workflow
  • 6. Careers in Data Science
  • 7. Bulding Strong Data Science Portfolios

II: Python and Math Fundamentals

  • 1. Python Programming Basics
  • 2. Python Stack for Data Science
  • 3. Single and Multivariable Calculus
  • 4. Fundamentals of Linear Algebra
  • 5. Statistics and Probability
  • 6. Introduction to Information Theory
  • 7. Logic, Algorithms and Graphs

III: Working with Data

  • 1. Data Structures and Algorithms
  • 2. Cloud Data Architectures
  • 3. Introduction to Descriptive Analytics
  • 4. Data Acquisition and Manipulation
  • 5. EDA and Statistical Inference
  • 6. Databases and SQL Fundamentals
  • 7. Business Data Analysis in SQL
  • 8. Data Prep with Paxata on DataRobot
  • 9. Storytelling: Communicating with Data
  • 10. Data Visualisation with Power BI

IV: Machine Learning

  • 1. Machine Learning: Pattern Discovery
  • 2. Machine Learning: Predictive Analytics
  • 3. Feature Engineering Best Practices
  • 4. Ensembles and Model Combination
  • 5. Evaluating Machine Learning Models
  • 6. Statistical Testing & ML Experiment Design
  • 7. Hands-on AutoML on DataRobot
  • 8. Scalable Analytics and ML on Big Data
  • 9. ML with Probabilistic Graphical Models
  • 10. Statistical Data Bias Detection

V: Building Data Solutions

  • 1. Time Series & Financial Data Analysis
  • 2. Introduction to Natural Language Processing
  • 3. Feature Engineering for NLP
  • 4. Machine Learning for NLP
  • 5. Text Mining and Sentiment Analysis
  • 6. Applied NLP: Chatbot Development
  • 7. Retail Analytics and Demand Forecasting
  • 8. Marketing and Customer Analytics

VI: Decision Analytics & Intelligence

  • 1. Prescriptive Analytics: Introduction to Decision Science
  • 2. Approaches to Prescriptive Analytics
  • 3. Decision Support: Influence Diagrams
  • 4. Decision Support: Argumentation
  • 5. Optimisation Models and Methods
  • 6. Pitching to Managers/Decision-Makers
  • 7. Understanding Stakeholder Management
  • 8. Decision Automation Systems

VII: Operationalising Data Products

  • 1. What is Data Product Management?
  • 2. Software Engineering Skills for Data Science
  • 3. Git, GitHub, and Collaboration
  • 4. Architectural Design Methodologies
  • 5. Introduction to TensorFlow and Pytorch
  • 6. Production Machine Learning Best Practices
  • 7. DataOps with Amazon Sagemaker
  • 8. Enterprise Model Risk Management
