ENTERPRISE DATA SCHOOL

Data Science and Business Analytics


Get intensive training for a successful career in big data, business intelligence, data product management and AI.

Uncover the magic of data science


The Data Science and Business Analytics Bootcamp equips you with the full range of skills for navigating sophisticated data science in light of complex business problems. This catalyst programme speeds up time-to-application so you can begin to tackle valuable problems in any organisation.

TOOLS YOU WILL LEARN

SQL for data analysis, DataRobot for machine learning, and Microsoft PowerBI for visualisation and business reporting.

Programme Outline

You will complete 16 modules in 4 parts, and a hands-on final project. Explore modules below

Part I: Data Science Fundamentals

  • 1. Introduction to Data and Analytics
  • 2. Business Thinking for Data Scientists
  • 3. Data Science Project Workflow
  • 4. Statistics and Exploratory Data Analysis

Part II: Working with Data

  • 5. Databases and SQL for Data Analytics
  • 6. Data Visualisation Best Practices with PowerBI
  • 7. Data Preparation with Paxata on DataRobot
  • 8. Feature Engineering & Pre-Modelling Best Practices

Part III: Building Data Solutions

  • 9. Machine Learning and Predictive Analytics
  • 10. Statistical Testing and ML Experiment Design
  • 11. Hands-on AutoML on DataRobot
  • 12. Marketing and Customer Analytics
  • 13. Data-Driven Decision Making

Part IV: Breaking into Data Science

  • 14. Exploring Data Science Careers
  • 15. Building Strong Data Science Portfolios
  • 16. Landing Data Jobs: From Projects to Interviews
  • Project: Build An End-to-End Data Solution
